Creating your first deck

At this point you have probably played a couple of matches using the default decks but you want to expand your horizons and build a deck of your own. Here are some tips on how to make a strong deck that you can be proud of!

Tip 1 – Mana Curve

An example of a smooth mana curve.

It’s important to make sure that you have cards to play throughout the game. A good way of regulating your cards to ensure this is possible to look at the decks Mana Curve. The Mana Curve is a bar chart that shows the Mana cost of all of the cards in the deck. In most decks you will want the Mana Curve to be smooth and not have harsh jumps and cliffs.

Tip 2 – Minions/Spells

You need to make sure your deck has enough minions. A purely spell deck will have many troubles. A good guideline when starting out is to have at least 20 minions in your deck. You can ignore this tip once you have a better grasp of deck building as it is not a rule only a guideline.

Tip 3 – Take Inspiration

Learn from other decks whenever you can. Try to figure out what makes your opponents deck strong and see if you can replicate it. The internet is also full of decks that you can draw inspiration from. Take a look at our deck lists section!
